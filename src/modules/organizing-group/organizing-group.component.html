Organizing Committee

The Cosmology and Gravitation Group, COSMOGRAV-UTA, is primarily dedicated to the study of the origin and evolution of the universe as a whole, utilizing as pillars theories of gravitation and quantum field physics. Additionally, natural phenomena originating from the dynamics of gravitational fields are studied within the context of Einstein's General Theory of Relativity and its various extensions. Some of the topics of interest for the group include:

Inflation | Reheating | Dark Matter and Dark Energy | Cosmological Perturbations | Structure Formation | Gravitational Waves

The group's research aims to deepen our understanding of the fundamental principles governing the universe and contribute to the broader field of cosmology and gravitational physics.
